Guhuza

Welcome to Guhuza

Guhuza is a web-based game that engages users through the job-seeking and hiring process.

Compete with other players, improve your skills, and experience the excitement of finding your dream job in a fun and interactive way!

How to Play

Click "Play" in the navigation bar. You'll be guided through various job-seeking scenarios.

Complete challenges, apply for positions, and compete with other players for the highest score!

Benefits of Playing

  • Improve your job-seeking skills
  • Network with other players
  • Win exciting prizes and rewards
  • Build your professional profile

Skills Development

Enhance your professional skills through interactive challenges and quests.

  • Specialized training exercises
  • Progress tracking
  • Skill assessments

Networking

Expand your professional network in a fun, gamified environment.

  • Connect with other job seekers
  • Join industry groups
  • Participate in events
